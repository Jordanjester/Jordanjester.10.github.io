Scope and Limitation

This research will be conducted to know the pros and cons of Grade 12 Senior High School learners on the use of generative artificial intelligence in education. Also, it will be conducted to know the risks and benefits of generative AI in education, and to know how the learners’ views on generative AI’s integration in education and in schools. This study starts on February 5, 2024 and will end on May 8, 2024 in Our Lady of Lourdes College Foundation. The participants of this study will be Grade 12 Senior High School learners, and Senior High School faculty members. This research will focus on knowing the pros and cons of using Generative Artificial Intelligence among Grade 12 Senior High School learners of Our Lady of Lourdes College Foundation. However, this research study will not cover other types of artificial intelligence that is used in other industries such as robotics, machineries, and many more. Also, this study will only interview ten (5) Grade 12 Senior High School learners in different strands of Our Lady of Lourdes College Foundation’s school premises.
