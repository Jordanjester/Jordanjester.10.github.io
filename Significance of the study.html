Signifance of the study

With the rapid evaluation of technology, it is crucial to understand how these advancements can impact and enhance our lives. This study helps to provide knowledgeable information to the following: Students, The results of the study will help students weigh the benefits and risks of incorporating generative artificial intelligence (AI) into their school works. Teachers, This study will inform educators of the benefits and risks of implementing generative artificial intelligence (AI) in education. Future Researchers, The conducted study will further open doors for future researchers to expand the studies about generative artificial intelligence (AI), this may also serve as a source of information for them. DEPED, The results of the study can give information on how to properly manage the use of Generative Artificial Intelligence (GAI). They can also create programs for students to give further knowledge about the topic.
